# Adding Your Epic Screenshot

To overlay the chatbot on your Epic EHR screenshot:

1. Save your Epic screenshot image file
2. Name it `epic-screenshot.png`
3. Place it in the `medical-research-chatbot` folder (same folder as `index.html`)

The chatbot widget will automatically overlay on top of your Epic screenshot when you open `index.html`.

## Supported Image Formats

The app expects a PNG file named `epic-screenshot.png`, but you can modify the HTML to use other formats:
- JPG/JPEG: Change `src="epic-screenshot.png"` to `src="epic-screenshot.jpg"`
- Any other format: Update the filename in `index.html` line 12

## Image Recommendations

- High resolution (1920x1080 or higher) for best quality
- Full Epic interface screenshot
- The chatbot widget will appear in the bottom-right corner and can be dragged around
